Day 1: Spider Database Website

In The Beginning...

"In the beginning of creation, when Marc made the database UI, the website was without form and void, with darkness over the face of the HTML, and a mighty script that swept over the surface of the IDE.  Marc said, 'Let there be a database spider UI', and there was a database spider UI; and Marc saw that the UI was good, and he separated primary keys from foreign keys.  He called the foreign keys parent/child relationships, and the darkness Ruby on Rails.  So evening came, and morning came, the first day."
